Ruel is a British-Australian singer and songwriter from Sydney. Ruel started his first tour right after his first album released in 2018, and his world tour later in 2019. With numbers of tours and music awards winning at age17, Ruel is definitely an eye catching for Australia's newest musical prodigy. His latest Album will be released on October 23,2020.
